◆ RING_BUFFER_IS_POWER_OF_TWO

#define RING_BUFFER_IS_POWER_OF_TWO ( buffer_size)    ((buffer_size & (buffer_size - 1)) == 0)

Checks if the buffer_size is a power of two.

Due to the design only RING_BUFFER_SIZE-1 items can be contained in the buffer. buffer_size must be a power of two.
